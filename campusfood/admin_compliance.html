Compliance Guidelines

Based on South African R146/2010 regulations, vendors must declare the following allergens: Peanuts, Tree Nuts, Milk/Dairy, Eggs, Soy, Fish, Shellfish, and Gluten. Dietary information (Halal, Vegan, Vegetarian, Gluten-Free) is also recommended for student safety.
